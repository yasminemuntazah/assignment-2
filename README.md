Rather than relying on a single source, the documentation synthesizes insights from leading practitioners across technical SEO, semantic search, AI search behavior, content strategy, enterprise SEO, and information retrieval.

These experts were selected because they consistently publish original research, speak at major industry conferences, build widely adopted SEO products, or actively shape how the industry understands AI-powered search.

# Research Philosophy

This repository does **not** aim to copy opinions from individual experts.

Instead, it compares recurring themes across multiple practitioners to identify emerging best practices in AI-powered search.

Whenever several independent experts arrive at similar conclusions—for example:

- Entity-first optimization
- AI visibility over rankings
- Original research as a competitive advantage
- Structured, machine-readable content
- Brand mentions across trusted sources

those ideas are treated as stronger evidence than any single opinion.

This approach helps build a continuously evolving knowledge base for SEO in the era of Large Language Models (LLMs).
