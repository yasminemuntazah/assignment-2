### **Transcript**
Search is evolving beyond simple keyword matching. Google is increasingly focused on understanding concepts and matching them with specific pieces of information, a process I call "tokenization."

This means search engines can now break down content—whether text, video, or audio—into discrete, smaller units to provide more precise answers. Instead of ranking an entire page, they can identify and surface specific sections that directly address a user's query.

This deeper understanding signifies a major shift from older SEO tactics. It underscores the importance of creating granular, high-quality content that feeds these sophisticated AI models. Your content is not just being indexed; it's being deconstructed to serve answers.
