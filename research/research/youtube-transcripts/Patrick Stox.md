# Overview

The search landscape is undergoing its biggest transformation since Google was introduced.

Traffic from traditional search is declining as users increasingly get answers directly from AI assistants such as:

- ChatGPT
- Google AI Overviews
- Gemini
- Claude
- Perplexity

SEO is no longer just about ranking pages. Instead, brands must optimize for **visibility across AI ecosystems**, improve their products, and become the preferred answer in AI-generated responses.

---

# Key Takeaways

## 1. AI Search Is Reducing Organic Clicks

Patrick highlights that AI-generated answers significantly reduce website traffic.

### Organic Click Decline

| Search Experience | Estimated CTR Drop |
|------------------|-------------------:|
| Early AI Overviews | ~34.5% |
| Recent AI Overviews | ~58% |
| AI Chatbots | <5% CTR |

Although traffic decreases, many businesses report:

- stable revenue
- stable conversions
- improved lead quality

### Why?

Users now:

1. Research inside AI assistants.
2. Compare options.
3. Visit only the final brand they're interested in.

Result:

Lower traffic but higher purchase intent.

---

# 2. Freshness Matters More Than Ever

Traditional SEO focused on:

- search volume
- keyword difficulty

AI search prioritizes:

- fresh information
- unique insights
- highly relevant content

Patrick notes that even pages with **zero Google traffic** can be cited by ChatGPT if they contain valuable, up-to-date information.

---

# 3. Relevance Beats Everything

AI doesn't evaluate entire pages.

Instead it retrieves:

- sentences
- paragraphs
- specific sections

The most important question becomes:

> Does this piece of content directly answer the user's question?

---

# 4. AI Search Attribution Is Broken

Current analytics cannot reliably measure AI traffic.

Problems include:

- missing referral data
- AI traffic appearing as "Direct"
- personalized AI responses
- inconsistent citations

Current best practices:

- monitor branded search growth
- compare AI visibility over time
- use self-attribution surveys at checkout
- track conversions rather than clicks
