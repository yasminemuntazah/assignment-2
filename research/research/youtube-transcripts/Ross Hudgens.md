# Overview

HubSpot began investing in **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** in early 2023, immediately after ChatGPT's breakthrough.

Their core thesis:

> **Discovery has shifted from search engines to Large Language Models (LLMs).**

Instead of optimizing solely for Google rankings, HubSpot now optimizes how AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI describe, recommend, and cite HubSpot.

---

# The Biggest Shift

## Traditional SEO

```
Search Query
↓

Google Ranking
↓

Website Visit
↓

Conversion
```

## AI Search

```
Question
↓

LLM Response

↓

Brand Mention

↓

Purchase Decision
```

Users increasingly make decisions **before ever visiting a website**.

---

# Discovery Has Moved to LLMs

According to HubSpot:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Google AI Mode

have become the first place users research software.

The question is no longer:

> "Can users find my website?"

Instead:

> **"What is AI saying about my brand?"**

---

# Visibility > Clicks

HubSpot's primary KPI is **AI Visibility**, not citations or traffic.

Instead of asking:

- How many clicks?
- How many visits?

They ask:

- How often is HubSpot mentioned?
- Which prompts trigger HubSpot?
- What does AI say about HubSpot?

---

# AI Traffic Is Underreported

Referral data from AI systems is still unreliable.

HubSpot relies heavily on:

- Self-attribution surveys
- Customer interviews
- Sales conversations

One major finding:

> Customers who evaluated HubSpot using AI have significantly higher purchase intent than customers coming from traditional channels.

# HubSpot's Blog Transformation

## The Challenge

HubSpot experienced a major decline in organic blog traffic.

Most lost traffic came from:

- informational searches
- educational keywords
- low-intent queries

Example:

```
"What is Content Marketing?"
```

AI now answers these questions directly.

---

## The Opportunity

Instead of treating this as a failure, HubSpot redesigned its content strategy.

Goal:

```
Website

↓

Influence AI

↓

Influence Buyers
```

Content now reaches users indirectly through AI-generated responses.

---

# B2B → B2B2C

HubSpot introduces a new concept:

```
Business

↓

Bot

↓

Consumer
```

Your first audience is no longer the customer.

Your first audience is the AI system.

# Write for LLMs, Not Just Humans

HubSpot now optimizes content for:

# TOFU Isn't Dead

Top-of-funnel content still matters.

However, it must change.

AJ calls it:

> **"TOFU with a twist."**

---

## Old TOFU

```
What is Email Marketing?

↓

Educational content

↓

CTA
```

---

## New TOFU

```
Explain Email Marketing

↓

Teach the concept

↓

Explicitly connect it to your product
```

Example:

Instead of saying:

> Segmentation improves email marketing.

Say:

> You can segment your audience using HubSpot Email Marketing.

This explicit product association helps LLMs connect the concept with your brand.

---

# Explicit Brand Associations

One major mistake companies make:

Provide excellent educational content...

...without ever mentioning how their product solves the problem.

LLMs then:

- summarize the content
- omit the company
- recommend competitors later

Always explain:

```
Problem

↓

Solution

↓

Your Product
```

# Query Fan-Out

LLMs answer complex questions by:

```
Question

↓

Break into subquestions

↓

Search multiple sources

↓

Combine answers

↓

Generate response
```

This explains why many niche websites appear in AI citations.

# Reddit Strategy

HubSpot treats Reddit carefully.

Rather than aggressively promoting itself,

they focus on:

- building their own subreddit
- encouraging genuine discussions
- creating active communities

Result:

Community members naturally mention HubSpot elsewhere on Reddit.

---

# Community Before Promotion

Successful Reddit strategy:

```
Give Value

↓

Build Trust

↓

Participate

↓

Mention Product Naturally
```

Never lead with promotion.

---

# Brands Don't Always Need Their Own Community

HubSpot recommends creating a dedicated Reddit community only when:

- discussions happen organically
- sufficient engagement exists

Otherwise:

Participate in existing communities instead.

# Final Thoughts

HubSpot's approach shows that **AEO is not simply SEO with AI keywords**.

It is a shift from optimizing webpages to optimizing **brand understanding across AI ecosystems**.

Winning in AI search requires:

- educational content that clearly links problems to your product,
- strong off-site brand presence,
- creator and affiliate ecosystems,
- community engagement,
- and measuring **AI visibility as a business metric**, not just rankings or clicks.
