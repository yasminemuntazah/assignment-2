# Overview

Traditional SEO is evolving.

Instead of focusing solely on ranking webpages, modern search optimization is increasingly about ensuring that **your brand, entity, and products become part of the AI's understanding of the web**.

Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, Claude, and Perplexity rely heavily on information gathered from multiple sources—not just your website.

# Entity Attribute Optimization

Instead of optimizing only keywords:

```
Best Survey Software
```

Optimize for attributes like:

- Best for Small Business
- Best Design
- Best Collaboration
- Best Security
- Best Enterprise
- Best Customer Support

LLMs classify brands based on these attributes.

Example:

```
Brand A
├── Fast
├── Cheap
├── Easy to Use
├── AI Features
└── Small Business
```

The more consistently these attributes appear across the web, the more likely AI systems are to recommend the brand.

# Third-Party Signals Matter More

Koray explains that many AI citations come from:

- Reddit
- Quora
- Forums
- News websites
- Reviews
- Guest posts
- Social media

Not necessarily from your own website.

Instead of only building backlinks:

Build **entity claims**.

# External Topical Authority

Traditional SEO focuses on topical authority within your own site.

Example:

```

SEO
├── Technical SEO
├── Local SEO
├── Ecommerce SEO
```

Koray introduces **External Topical Authority**.

```
Website
      ↓

News Site
Forum
Medium
Reddit
Quora
Industry Blog
Social Media
```

Every external publication reinforces your expertise.

Goal:

Create a consistent topical footprint across the open web.

# Three Types of External Signals

Third-party publications generate:

## 1. Backlinks

Traditional SEO signal.

---

## 2. Mentions

Brand name appears without links.

---

## 3. Claims ⭐

The strongest signal.

Examples:

> Company X is the best CRM for startups.

> Brand Y offers the fastest QR code generator.

These declarations help LLMs classify your entity.

# Consensus Engineering

One important concept discussed is **consensus**.

Instead of publishing information once:

Repeat the same facts across multiple trusted sources.

Example

```

↓

Site B

↓

Medium

↓

Reddit

↓

News
```

Every source reinforces the same entity definition.

Koray calls this creating a "surround sound" effect.

# Reddit Optimization

Reddit remains extremely influential.

Suggested workflow:

1. Find Reddit threads ranking for target keywords.
2. Identify relevant subreddits.
3. Build account reputation (karma).
4. Participate naturally.
5. Publish useful content.
6. Gradually influence discussions.

# Why Listicles Work

LLMs frequently cite listicles because they are:

- structured
- easy to aggregate
- inexpensive to process

Example

```
Top 10 VPNs

Best Survey Software

Best CRM

Best QR Generator
```

Google and LLMs can quickly compare multiple entities from these pages.

# LLM Crawling Behavior

LLMs appear to:

- crawl many websites
- maintain their own indexes
- prioritize trusted domains
- use domain authority as one ranking factor

However,

they also crawl:

- smaller websites
- forums
- blogs
- newly discovered domains

# Freshness Signals

Koray mentions that dates inside URLs may influence crawling priority.

Example

```

```

Fresh URLs may receive faster crawling.

# Semantic Optimization

Writing style matters.

Recommendations:

- Short sentences
- Clear statements
- Less opinion
- More facts
- More numbers
- Validate claims
- Use consistent terminology

---

# Micro Semantics

LLMs analyze word selection.

Example

Instead of

> Good coffee machine.

Write

> Coffee machine with stainless steel housing.

Specific vocabulary increases semantic relevance.

- reinforce correct attributes
- repeat information consistently
- outnumber incorrect information
