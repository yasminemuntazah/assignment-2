## AI Search Is Becoming Hyper-Personalized

**Host**

What is your biggest focus regarding AI Search?

**Michael King**

## What Does This Mean for SEO?

**Host**

How will this affect rankings?

**Michael King**

SEO will become much more **persona-driven**. One piece of content will no longer serve everyone. Businesses must create content tailored to specific audiences to remain competitive in search.

## The Future of Search

**Host**

What do you expect over the next 6–12 months?

**Michael King**

The focus should shift from SEO to **search**.

Many SEO tasks are repetitive ETL processes (Extract, Transform, Load) that AI can automate. Instead of resisting automation, SEO professionals should embrace AI and concentrate on delivering uniquely human value.

# Key Insights

1. **Search is becoming AI-driven and highly personalized.** Traditional search and AI Search are merging, with results increasingly tailored to individual users.
2. **SEO strategies must evolve.** Businesses should create audience-specific content rather than relying on one generic page for everyone.
3. **AI will automate many SEO tasks.** Repetitive processes like reporting, data collection, and optimization will become increasingly automated, allowing professionals to focus on strategy.
4. **Success depends on adapting to new technologies.** SEO professionals should learn from technologies developed by Google, OpenAI, and Anthropic while continuously experimenting with AI tools.
5. **The future of SEO is driven by evidence, not theory.** The industry should prioritize real-world data, experiments, and case studies over speculation.
